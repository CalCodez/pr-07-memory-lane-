    ABOUT

    For those of you who don’t know, my name is Calvin Cortez Colbert. I was born April 7th, 1987 in Wichita, Ks. I was raised by my grandmother Gloria Dean Colbert who passed away on August 11th, 2011. I grew up very sheltered and spoiled, but hey, that’s my business.

    I’m an introverted music lover and I’d like to think of myself as an “all things” positive type of person. Peace, love, and positivity used to be a driving force in my life. I found that in the world we lived in finding all three was seemingly impossible unless I was dealing with.. well… myself. I knew this long before I knew what an “introvert” was.

    Things they say about introverts are things like weird, socially awkward, and other adjectives they would throw in the mix that don’t matter. Introvert just simply means, I entertain who I entertain, and don’t who I don’t but through it all, I’ve always and will always fuck with me. If you can make it past the barrier then congratulations, but if you didn’t/don’t, that’s nobody’s fault but yours, and that’s not to be taken as an insult or thought that we think we are better it’s all about vibes and criteria. Energy, really. Either your energy is conducive or draining to my mental, social, and interpersonal battery.

    With all that being said… sometimes. It may just take time for us to find the vibe in you. Unlike extroverts who have a natural ability to carry a room with remarkable people skills… That is a lot of work for an introvert.

    Growing up I’ve always been the type to be in my room vibing out to music. Singing, dancing, writing, getting lost in creative type things. Making up songs, dance routines, writing short stories, or just messing around on the computer. Content beyond reason.

    In my younger years, all my “friends” were all females. I never really could put it together as to why I always gravitated towards and found the most comfort among females. It was a thing though. Not really going to get into it all… maybe another project. It’s not really important. Just wanted to mention it for the sake to stating that I wasn’t just like a hermit or nothing like that. Anyhow.

    WHY

    Now to the question… Why? What is this all about?
    I mentioned earlier that my grandmother who raised me passed away in August on 2011. The reason was to established a timeline on the mess you will see in this project. For context.

    Again, not going to really dive into fine details. But the day of her funeral I was noticing things that I never really paid attention to as far as the actions in other people. So, from this point on, I’m just going to say that is has been hell on wheels ever since and still to this day… The devil and his minions stay sucking on my balls!...

    CONTENT

    Instead of getting into it all day by day, year by year, or event by event. Luckily, I was using some decompressing methods that I was able to capture by ranting in free writing styles and videos. In this project, I will navigate you through. So it will make more sense when the time comes. If you should choose to explore.

    At the end of the day this content is all about Me and my experiences. How other choose to feel is 100% irrelevant in any logical sense on earth. So all I can do for those matters is offer my ass to kiss... free of charge.
